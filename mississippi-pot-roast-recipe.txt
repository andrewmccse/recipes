Ingredients:
	- 2-4 lb chuck roast
	- packet of range mix
	- packet of au jus gravy mix
	- pepperonccinis
	- 1/4 cup butter

Directions:
	1. Brown rost in skillet on high heat.

	2. coat roast with ranch and gravy mix inside electric pressure cooker.

	3. add butter and pepperonccinis.

	4. cook for 45 minutes and natural release for 30 minutes.
